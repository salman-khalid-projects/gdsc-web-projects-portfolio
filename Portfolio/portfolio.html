About Me

I am a Front End web Developer.

My name is Salman Khalid. I am from University of Bahrain as a software engineering student. My experience is in the frontend web development field and my next goal is to become a full stack web developer. Currently, I am working on my senior project - handeling the management/frontend parts - to develop a database visualization tool that enables users to manage database systems with minimum knowledge about databases. Also I am learning various programming languages like Java, python and C++. In addition, some courses related to full stack web development and related frameworks like Angular, React and Next.js.

My interests lie mostly in sports. I love practicing sports every morning. My top sports are running, football and table tennis. I love volunteering and helping others whenever I have the chance. I am the lead of the (GDSC) University of Bahrain 2021/2022 team which aims to help university students to inhance their knowledge and practical work in many IT fields.
